What I'm doing

I am currently writing my PhD thesis with Julien Grivaux and Damien Calaque, on the subject of twisting cochains in complex geometry and Chern classes in Deligne cohomology.

What I've done

I graduated with an MMath (Mathematics) from the University of Oxford in 2016, after having finished my BA in Mathematics there. I wrote my main master's thesis (a translation and reader's guide to Toën and Vaquié's Au-dessous de Spec Z) under the supervision of Kobi Kremnitzer and Christopher Hollings, as well as an additional (unsupervised but examined) thesis on persistent homology.
