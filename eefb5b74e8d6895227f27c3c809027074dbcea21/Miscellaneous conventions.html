Miscellaneous conventions

(other conventions: Simplicial conventions, Stiefel manifolds, Chern and Atiyah things)

  • Vector bundles have...
    • a left action (when viewed as a principal GL-bundle);
    • ψα:π−1(Uα)−→∼Uα×ℂr;
    • gαβ=ψα∘ψ−1β:Uβ×ℂr→Uα×ℂr;
    • gαβsβ=sα for sections sα(x)↔(x,σα(x)).
  • dlogM:=M−1dM for a matrix M.
    • generally, M/N:=N−1M (although we try to avoid this notation...) (but used to 'make sense of' e.g. Čech cohomology)
  • for endomorphism-valued forms we write ∧Ω and ∧↺ to differentiate between the wedge product on the form part and on the endomorphism part, and we use the latter for the vector space wedge too (e.g. for A∈Ω1⊗Mr×r(ℂ) we write dA(xi)∧↺dA(xj) to mean that the wedge is still on the 'vectorial' part, not on the forms)
  • we never write the wedge product of differential forms as α∧β (except maybe when we define simplicial differential forms, but then we say that we won't write this anymore) but instead just write αβ, or maybe α⋅β if needed; the wedge product ∧ is reserved solely for endomorphisms
