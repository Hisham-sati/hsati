Terminology and fundamentals

Chern 'things'

Rank-r vector bundle E on dim-n space X

  • Chern classes c0(E)=1 and c1(E),…,cr(E) defined somehow (e.g. Chern-Weil or via a universal bundle etc.)
  • Total Chern class c(E)=∑ici(E)
  • Chern polynomial ct(E)=∑ici(E)ti; roots are Chern roots ai(E)
  • Exponential Chern classes ch0(E)=r and ch1(E),…,chn(E) with chi(E):=Pi(c1(E),…,cr), where the Pi are the polynomials that turn the elementary symmetric polynomials into the power-sum symmetric polynomials, defined below
  • Total exponential Chern class, or Chern character ch(E)=∑ichi(E)

Symmetric polynomials

  • Symmetric polynomials p(x1,…,xℓ) invariant under the action of Sℓ
  • Elementary symmetric polynomials σk(x1,…,xℓ) defined by ∏ℓk=1(x+tk)=∑ℓk=1σk(x1,…,xℓ)xℓ−k and σk(x1,…,xℓ)=0 for k>ℓ
  • Power-sum symmetric polynomials τk(x1,…,xℓ)=1k!(xk1+…+xkℓ)
  • Fundamental theorem of symmetric polynomials tells us that there exist Pk and Qk such that τk(x⎯⎯)=Pk(σ1(x⎯⎯),…,σk(x⎯⎯)) and σk(x⎯⎯)=Qk(τ1(x⎯⎯),…,τk(x⎯⎯)) where x⎯⎯=(x1,…,xℓ)

Relations

  • ch(E)=r+c1+12(c21−2c2)+16(c31−3c1c2+3c3)+…
  • If E=L1⊕…⊕Lr then ai(E)=c1(Li)
  • ck(E)=σk(a1(E),…,ak(E))=Qk(ch1(E),…,chk(E))
  • ch(L)=exp(c1(L)):=∑∞i=0c1(L)ii!

K-theory

The total Chern class is an additive group morphism from K-theory into the cohomology of X with coefficients in ℚ; the total exponential Chern class is a ring morphism from K-theory into the cohomology of X with coefficients in ℚ. That is,

c(E⊕F)c(E⊗F)ch(E⊕F)ch(E⊗F)=c(E)⌣c(F)=?=ch(E)+ch(F)=ch(E)⌣ch(F)

Axiomatic

  • Classical. ck∈H2k(X;ℤ) for complex vector bundles over a topological space
    1. c0(E)=1
    2. ck(f∗E)=f∗ck(E) for continuous f
    3. ck(E⊕F)=∑ki=0ci(E)⌣ck−i(F); i.e. c(E⊕F)=c(E)⌣c(F)
    4. the total Chern class of the tautological line bundle over ℂℙk is 1−H, where H is Poincaré dual to ℂℙk−1⊂ℂℙk
  • Grothendieck
    1. ck(f∗E)=f∗ck(E) for continuous f
    2. c(E)=c(E′)⌣c(E″) for any short exact sequence 0→E′→E→E″→0
    3. c(L)=1+e(Lℝ), where e(Lℝ) is the Euler class of the underlying real vector bundle
