Stiefel manifolds

'My' notation and conventions

  • Wn,k:=GLn(ℂ)GLn−k(ℂ)
    (N.B. we use GL rather than U) is the space of k-frames in ℂn (as opposed to the U(n) definition, which gives orthonormal k-frames).
  • Wn,k has a trivial rank-??? sub-bundle
  • We usually work (for transgression) with Wn,k−1 — have to be careful to not make degree mistakes because of this!
  • We transgress ck of a rank-r bundle to H2k−1(Wn,n−k+1;ℤ)

Other notations

  • In [BM96] they write Wn,k to mean what we call Wn,n−k, i.e.
    WBMn,k:=GLn(ℂ)GLk(ℂ).
