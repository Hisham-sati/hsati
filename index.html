What I'm doing

I'm currently writing my PhD thesis with Julien Grivaux and Damien Calaque, on the subject of twisting cochains in complex geometry and Chern classes of coherent sheaves in Deligne cohomology.

What I've done

I graduated with an MMath (Mathematics) from the University of Oxford in 2016, after having finished my BA in Mathematics there. I wrote my main master's thesis (a translation and reader's guide to Toën and Vaquié's Au-dessous de Spec Z) under the supervision of Kobi Kremnitzer and Christopher Hollings, as well as a thesis on the subject of persistent homology. Both can be found on the papers page.

For more details see my [CV].

Research interests

Homotopy theory, higher algebra, derived algebraic geometry, quantum computing, type theory, birds.
